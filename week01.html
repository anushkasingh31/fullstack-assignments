Lord Krishna, the eighth incarnation of Lord Vishnu, is one of the most revered deities in Hinduism. He is known for his wisdom, charm, and divine playfulness. Born in Mathura to Devaki and Vasudeva, Krishna's childhood was filled with miracles and adventures, including lifting the Govardhan Hill and defeating demons.

Festivals like Janmashtami are dedicated to his birth, and his teachings continue to inspire millions across the world. His message of love, selflessness, and Dharma remains relevant in every era.
